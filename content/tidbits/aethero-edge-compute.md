Most satellites collect data and beam it down for processing somewhere else. Aethero wants to do
the work up there. Its March 11, 2024 FCC license application covers a satellite called Deimos,
carrying electro-optical sensors and an onboard processor the company wants to prove out in
space. The mission, the filing says, would "demonstrate how on-orbit edge computing can
facilitate the efficient processing of electro-optical data," with insights produced onboard
for tasks like mapping and resource exploration.

It is a small, specific ask: one demonstration satellite, Deimos, built to certify the onboard
processor and show what it can do with electro-optical data in orbit.

> "demonstrate how on-orbit edge computing can facilitate the efficient processing of electro-optical data"
> — Aethero, FCC license application
